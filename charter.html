The Digital Charter of the Kingdom of Prima Syam

This Charter outlines the structure, rights, and responsibilities within the Digital Kingdom of Prima Syam.

  • Article 1 - Name: The nation shall be known as the Kingdom of Prima Syam.
  • Article 2 - Sovereignty: Sovereignty rests with the Sultan, guided by wisdom and deliberation.
  • Article 3 - Government: The Sultan leads the kingdom supported by advisory councils and ministries.
  • Article 4 - Citizens: Citizens include all who pledge allegiance to the Kingdom through digital registration.
  • Article 5 - Law: The law combines cultural tradition, universal human rights, and digital ethics.
  • Article 6 - Economy: The kingdom maintains a digital economy with a national token and treasury.

This Charter is effective from the day of its declaration and may evolve through royal and public consultation.
