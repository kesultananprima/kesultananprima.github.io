Meaning of the Emblem of the Kingdom of Prima Syam

The emblem of the Kingdom is shaped like a palace composed of six color pillars:

  • YMTS (Black): Represents the roof, the overarching protection and authority.
  • YMR (Gold/Yellow): The strong and straight structure of the palace â€” resilience and nobility.
  • YMP Cahaya (Red): The strongest pillar, symbolizing strength and leadership in all matters.
  • YMP Mutia (Green): A pillar of calm and harmony, like the vast and nurturing earth.
  • YMP Aisyah (Blue): The embodiment of compassion, softness, and loving hearts.
  • YMP Imam (Orange): Represents the pillar of Islamic faith and spiritual guidance.
